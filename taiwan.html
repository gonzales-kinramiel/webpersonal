Taiwan

Why I Want to Go to Taiwan

Taiwan is known for its delicious street food, beautiful landscapes, and rich history. From the towering Taipei 101 to the scenic Sun Moon Lake, Taiwan offers diverse experiences for travelers.

How to Get There

You can fly to Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport, which connects to major destinations worldwide.

Other Places to Visit in Taiwan

  • Taroko Gorge - A breathtaking marble canyon with hiking trails.
  • Shilin Night Market - Famous for Taiwanese street food.
  • Alishan - A scenic mountain area known for its sunrise views and tea plantations.
